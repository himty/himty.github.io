[Team] PacNyan


It turns out that the best way to rack up a huge score is to run past scared ghosts in home. Broken, much?

In a team of three, I worked on PacNyan, a Nyan Cat-themed spinoff of PAC-MAN, for my final APCS project. Just as in the original game, you (the nyan cat) must eat all the dots to win the game. Each of the ghosts have their own personalities. Large dots temporarily scare the ghosts and make them return home by following a hard-coded flow map. You have three lives before you're out!
